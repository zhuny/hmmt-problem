# Time Independent Schrodinger Equation
Solving by the method of separation of variables $\Psi (x, t) = \psi(x) \phi(t)$

$$\begin{align}\phi(t) &= e^{-iEt/\hbar} \\ -\frac{h^2}{2m}\frac{d^2 \phi}{d x^2} + V\Phi &= E\Phi\end{align}$$
